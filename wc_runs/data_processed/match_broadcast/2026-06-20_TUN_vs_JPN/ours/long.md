## ⑦ 看点 · 纪录 · 花絮
- **Historic Milestone**: The 1,000th match in FIFA World Cup history, dating back to the inaugural 1930 tournament's 18 matches.  
- **Asian Record**: Japan became the first AFC nation to score four goals in a World Cup match in 151 previous attempts, registering the largest victory ever for an Asian side in tournament history.  
- **Official Commemoration**: Romanian referee Istvan Kovacs and his team wore special adidas kits with gold sleeve stripes and a "Match 1000" patch, selected on merit by FIFA Chief Refereeing Officer Pierluigi Collina.  
- **Managerial Turmoil**: Tunisia's defeat came after sacking Sabri Lamouchi following a 5-1 opening loss to Sweden, marking Renard's third World Cup as coach after Morocco (2018) and Saudi Arabia (2022).  
- **Head-to-Head**: Japan extended their historical dominance to 5 wins in 6 meetings, with Tunisia's sole victory coming in the 2022 Kirin Cup final. Their only prior World Cup encounter was Japan's 2-0 win in 2002.  
- **Standout Performance**: Ayase Ueda (Feyenoord) scored twice and assisted once, with his brace showcasing both long-range power and aerial precision, carrying his Eredivisie-leading club form to the world stage.
